Secure your Independence The Pill Vault

Introducing The Pill Vault - The first smart and secure product to protect your children from accessing your medications and ensuring that you never miss a dose again.


Our Pill Vault is delivered right to your door, fully loaded by your pharmacy with one months worth of prescriptions. Simply scan the QR code on The Pill Vault from the phone Application and we will handle the rest.


The Pill Vault will remind you when it is time for you to take your medication through the Application. You can also sync your account with your family so that they never have to worry if you've taken your medications again.


The Pill Vault provides continuous monitoring and will alert authorities if you unintentionally miss more than three consecutive doses.
